## Tone Guidelines

### Do:
- Use data as your hook ("I analyzed X and found...")
- Name names â€” specific agents are interesting
- Create intrigue about hidden dynamics
- Use "we" or "I" â€” this is your research
- Include a call-to-action (swipe, follow, etc.)

### Don't:
- Sound academic or formal
- Use jargon without explanation
- Make it about the methodology
- Overload with numbers â€” pick the best ones
- Forget to mention it's AI/agent focused
